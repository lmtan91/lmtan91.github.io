A Brief History Of Me

Caltech Postdoctoral Scholar
Jet Propulsion Laboratory & California Institute of Technology

My research focuses on gravitational-wave astrophysics in the context of pulsar-timing and ground-based laser interferometers. I have developed new algorithms to rapidly search for single sources, to map the amplitude and phase content of the background, and to mine the background for information about the astrophysical environment of sources.
